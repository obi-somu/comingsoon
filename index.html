The ethos of Mövenpick, established by Ueli Prager in 1948, emphasizes making high quality food and drink accessible and affordable for everyone. Innovations like à la carte dishes and fine wines by the glass helped broaden its appeal.

Mövenpick redefined fine dining by blending enjoyment with sophistication. This innovative approach has evolved into a premium international hotel brand that emphasizes culinary excellence and personal connection.
